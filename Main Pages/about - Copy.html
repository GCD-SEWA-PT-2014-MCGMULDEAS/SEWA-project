Who we are

GFC-STATS.com is a group project run as part of a submission for a course in Software Engineering for Web Applications in Griffith College Dublin. The team of Cathal, Gavin and Frank have developed the concept to complement the growing number of sites dedicated to sports analysis and GAA sports in particular. The site will evolve as more data becomes available and more in depth comparisons can be made.

How it works

To gain access to the analysis section a user must login and register on the site. Once registration is completed, an option is available to input personal data on training and matches based on individual dates. Thus a user can maintain a comprehensive record of their training activities and match performance indicators over time, enablisng them to identify and focus on training activities that will enhance their overall performance.
